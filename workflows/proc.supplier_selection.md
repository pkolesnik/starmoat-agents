# Strategic Sourcing & Supplier Selection  `proc.supplier_selection`

> function: `procurement` · value stage: Business Operations · optional · stage: series_a · default execution: `human`

**Why you need this:** Run a structured evaluation to choose the best-fit supplier for a given need.

**What good looks like:** Material purchases follow a documented selection with scored criteria and a clear award rationale.
