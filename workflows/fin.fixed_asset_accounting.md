# Fixed-Asset Accounting  `fin.fixed_asset_accounting`

> function: `finance_ops` · value stage: Business Operations · optional · stage: scale · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Tracks capital assets and depreciation so the balance sheet and tax records stay accurate.

**What good looks like:** The asset register reconciles to the GL and depreciation posts automatically each period.

## KPIs
- register-to-GL variance
- depreciation accuracy %
