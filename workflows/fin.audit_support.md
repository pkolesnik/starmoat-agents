# Audit Support & Compliance Reporting  `fin.audit_support`

> function: `finance_ops` · value stage: Business Operations · optional · stage: scale · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Prepares evidence and reports for external auditors and regulators so audits close cleanly.

**What good looks like:** Auditor requests are fulfilled from a ready evidence library with no material findings.
