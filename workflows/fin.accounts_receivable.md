# Accounts Receivable  `fin.accounts_receivable`

> function: `finance_ops` · value stage: Business Operations · **essential** · stage: seed · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Records customer payments and applies cash so receivables and the ledger stay accurate.

**What good looks like:** Cash is applied same-day and AR ties to the GL at every close with no aging surprises.
