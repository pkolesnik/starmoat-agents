# Engineering Skills and Capacity Planning  `eng.engineering_skills_capacity`

> function: `engineering` · value stage: Delivery & Execution · optional · stage: scale · default execution: `human`

**Why you need this:** Match engineering demand to team skills and capacity, surfacing gaps and hiring needs.

**What good looks like:** Work is staffed by people with the right skills; capacity gaps are visible early.

## KPIs
- demand vs capacity coverage
- skill gap closure time
