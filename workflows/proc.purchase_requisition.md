# Purchase Requisition & PO Management  `proc.purchase_requisition`

> function: `procurement` · value stage: Business Operations · **essential** · stage: foundational · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Convert approved requisitions into purchase orders and distribute them to suppliers.

**What good looks like:** Every committed spend has a PO raised against an approved requisition before the supplier delivers.

## KPIs
- % spend with PO
- PO creation cycle time
