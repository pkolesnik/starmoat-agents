# Purchase Approvals  `proc.purchase_approvals`

> function: `procurement` · value stage: Business Operations · optional · stage: series_a · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Route purchases through the right approvals and enforce budget policy.

**What good looks like:** Spend approved on policy with a clean audit trail.

- Approval cycle time
- Out-of-policy spend
