# Supplier Qualification & Risk Assessment  `proc.supplier_qualification`

> function: `procurement` · value stage: Business Operations · **essential** · stage: series_a · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Vet and certify suppliers for security, financial, and compliance risk before onboarding.

**What good looks like:** Every new supplier passes a risk and security review with documented sign-off before first PO.

## KPIs
- % suppliers qualified before PO
- open supplier risk items
