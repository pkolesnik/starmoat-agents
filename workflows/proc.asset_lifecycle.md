# IT Asset & Device Lifecycle Management  `proc.asset_lifecycle`

> function: `procurement` · value stage: Business Operations · **essential** · stage: seed · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Track devices and equipment from provisioning through retirement and secure disposal.

**What good looks like:** Every device is inventoried, assigned, and wiped/recovered at offboarding with a full audit trail.

## KPIs
- assets reclaimed at offboarding %
- asset inventory accuracy
