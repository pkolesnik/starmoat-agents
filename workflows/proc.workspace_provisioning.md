# Workspace & Facilities Provisioning  `proc.workspace_provisioning`

> function: `procurement` · value stage: Business Operations · optional · stage: seed · default execution: `human`

**Why you need this:** Acquire and set up workspace and facilities so teams have what they need to work.

**What good looks like:** New hires and teams get functional, equipped workspace on day one with no facilities gaps.

## KPIs
- workspace ready by start date %
- facilities ticket time
