# Supplier Performance Management  `proc.supplier_performance`

> function: `procurement` · value stage: Business Operations · optional · stage: scale · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Measure and review supplier performance against SLAs and quality expectations.

**What good looks like:** Key suppliers have scorecards reviewed on a cadence with corrective actions tracked.

## KPIs
- supplier SLA attainment %
- scorecard review cadence
