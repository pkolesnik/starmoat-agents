# Access & Identity Management  `secit.access_management`

> function: `security_it` · value stage: Business Operations · **essential** · stage: foundational · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Right access for the right people — provisioned on hire, revoked on exit, reviewed regularly.

**What good looks like:** Least-privilege enforced; periodic reviews with no dormant access.

## KPIs
- Access review completion
- Dormant accounts
- Provisioning time
