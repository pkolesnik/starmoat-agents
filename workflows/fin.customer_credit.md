# Customer Credit Management  `fin.customer_credit`

> function: `finance_ops` · value stage: Business Operations · optional · stage: series_a · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Sets and enforces credit terms so the company extends credit without taking outsized loss risk.

**What good looks like:** New accounts are credit-scored to policy and limits are reviewed before terms are granted.

## KPIs
- bad debt %
- credit approval cycle time
