# SaaS Provisioning & Deprovisioning  `secit.saas_provisioning`

> function: `security_it` · value stage: Business Operations · optional · stage: series_a · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Grant and revoke least-privilege access to SaaS applications across the employee lifecycle.

**What good looks like:** App access maps to role, is granted on day one, and fully revoked on departure.

## KPIs
- Access-revocation lag
- Least-privilege adherence
