# Profitability Reporting  `fin.profitability_reporting`

> function: `finance_ops` · value stage: Business Operations · optional · stage: scale · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Measures profitability by customer and product to guide pricing and mix decisions.

**What good looks like:** Profit-by-segment dashboards drive at least one pricing or mix decision per quarter.

## KPIs
- margin by segment
- loss-making accounts %
