# Succession Planning  `ppl.succession_planning`

> function: `people` · value stage: Business Operations · optional · stage: scale · default execution: `human`

**Why you need this:** Identify and develop successors for critical roles to ensure leadership continuity and reduce key-person risk.

**What good looks like:** Every critical role has a ready-now or ready-soon successor and bench strength is reviewed each cycle.

## KPIs
- Critical roles with named successor %
- Bench readiness
