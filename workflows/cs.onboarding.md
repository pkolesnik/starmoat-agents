> function: `customer_success` · value stage: Go-To-Market · **essential** · stage: foundational · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Time-to-value on a new customer determines whether they ever renew.

**What good looks like:** Every new customer hits first value on a tracked, on-time plan.

- Onboarding completion rate
