# Vendor Intake & Management  `proc.vendor_management`

> function: `procurement` · value stage: Business Operations · optional · stage: seed · default execution: `hybrid`

**What good looks like:** New vendors diligenced and approved through a consistent process.

- High-risk vendors flagged
