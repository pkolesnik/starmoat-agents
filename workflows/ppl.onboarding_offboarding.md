# Employee Onboarding & Offboarding  `ppl.onboarding_offboarding`

> function: `people` · value stage: Business Operations · **essential** · stage: foundational · default execution: `hybrid`

**Why you need this:** Get new hires productive fast and offboard cleanly (access, payroll, assets).

**What good looks like:** Day-one readiness on hire; complete, secure offboarding on exit.

## KPIs
- Time to productivity
- Offboarding completion

## Powered by (candidate agents)
- `employee-onboarding`

## Recommended tools
google, slack

## Handoffs (the canonical company flow)
- → `secit.access_management` (trigger: provision/deprovision access)
- → `fin.payroll` (trigger: payroll change)
