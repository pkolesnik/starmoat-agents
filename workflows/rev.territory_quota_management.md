# Sales Territory & Quota Management  `rev.territory_quota_management`

> function: `revenue_gtm` · value stage: Go-To-Market · optional · stage: scale · default execution: `human`

**Why you need this:** Allocate territories and set individual quotas so coverage and goals are balanced fairly across the sales team.

**What good looks like:** Every rep has a defined territory and an attainable quota tied to overall sales goals, reviewed each planning cycle.
