# Service Channel Strategy  `cs.channel_strategy`

> function: `customer_success` · value stage: Customer Intelligence · optional · stage: scale · default execution: `human`

**Why you need this:** Decide which support channels to offer and how to route customers to the right one.

**What good looks like:** Customers reach the optimal channel for their issue with measured cost-to-serve per channel.

## KPIs

- channel deflection rate
